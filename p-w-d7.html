MR Grade Plywood

  • Features
  • Description
  • Pictures
  • Moisture resistance: MR plywood is designed to be resistant to moisture and damp environments. It's made with a special water-resistant glue that prevents the plywood from soaking up water, expanding, or dissolving. This makes it ideal for use in places like kitchens and bathrooms.
  • Strength and stability MR plywood is made with hardwood veneers that provide strength and stability, even when tested against heavy loads. It's also warp-resistant, which ensures long-term durability.
  • Durability: MR plywood is strong and stable, even when tested against heavy loads. It's also warp-resistant, which ensures the structural stability and long-term durability of objects made from it.
  • Cost-effective: MR plywood is generally less expensive than other types of moisture-resistant plywood, like BWR (Boiling Water Resistant) or marine plywood.
  • Versatile: MR plywood is a good choice for a variety of applications, including interior furniture, kitchen cabinets, wardrobes, partitions, wall paneling, and indoor ceilings.
  • Wide availability: MR plywood is readily available in multiple sizes and widths.
  • Economical: MR plywood is a good balance of performance and affordability.

MR grade plywood is locally known as commercial plywood. This plywood makes an important ingredient for all types of furniture as it is the most commonly used, popular plywood among the construction industry. While most of us blindly go for the plywood suggested by the plywood seller, few of us would really like to know in entirety, about whatever we bring home. This blog is for those few people who care about their furniture just as much as we do. Before you buy plywood for your space, here is all you need to know about MR grade plywood :
It is not waterproof:
It's the most common misconception about MR grade plywood that it is waterproof. If you're looking for waterproof property in MR grade, you are sure to be disappointed because the full form of MR in MR grade plywood is moisture-resistant, meaning it can resist moisture with ease. However, it can only resist moisture and not water. Because of its moisture-resistant property, MR grade plywood is the most preferred kind of plywood in hot and humid conditions, mainly in tropical areas. CenturyPly offers a wide range of products in both MR grade and BWP (Boiling Water Proof) grade to meet your requirements.
It is not BWP or BWR grade plywood
Boiling waterproof plywood or boiling water-resistant plywood is far from MR grade plywood as these have a huge threshold for water exposure. This is the reason why they are commonly used in kitchens and bathrooms where more water-related activities are undertaken.
It is termite and borer resistant
Owing to the chemical treatment that good plywood companies use in making this plywood, MR grade plywood is made to resist termite and borer to a huge extent.
It is highly durable
Plywood with more number of plies has longer chances to sustain the wear and tear that occurs due to daily usage. MR grade plywood is made up of superior quality hardwood timber that gives it optimum strength and durability, best suited for commercial or residential use. These factors about MR grade plywood make it the number one choice for homeowners and architects. Brands like CenturyPly use the GLP formula or glue line protection formula as a preservative to protect the plywood from borer and termite. Good adhesion of plies is also important to keep plywood going for a long period of time. Above all, good quality MR plywood caters to distinct furniture needs and having an insight into the world of MR grade can help you pick the right one.
