  • Strength: Hardwood plywood is made of multiple layers of wood veneers glued together, making it very strong and sturdy. It's often used for heavy-duty projects like floors, walls, and furniture.
  • Durability: Hardwood plywood is resistant to damage and wear, and has high impact resistance
  • Stiffness: Hardwood plywood is stiff and has high planar shear strength.
  • Natural beauty: Hardwood plywood showcases the natural beauty of wood, with beautiful grains and textures.
  • Cost-effective: Hardwood plywood is a cost-effective alternative to solid wood.
  • Uses Hardwood plywood is used for projects that require a durable frame, such as furniture, musical instruments, wood casings, and sports equipment.

Hardwood plywood is a versatile, engineered wood product made from layers of wood veneer (usually from hardwood trees like oak, maple, birch, or walnut) that are glued together with the grain of each layer perpendicular to the next. This cross-graining method adds strength and reduces the natural tendency of wood to split, shrink, or warp.
HARDWOOD PLYWOOD USES
Hardwood Plywood is used in millwork for the crafting of furniture, cabinets, chests, built-in closets, kitchens, architectural applications, doors, paneling, toys, musical instruments, sporting equipment, skateboards etc. Most Hardwood plywood is made with Formaldehyde free glues which are not moisture resistant and are therefore Interior Grade panels. Certain specialty applications such as the Marine Industry, use exterior grade Hardwood Plywood which is available by special order. Many thicknesses, sizes, and grades are available.
