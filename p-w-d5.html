Decorative plywood-

Kharar - Kurali Rd, Aujala,Kharar

  • Description
  • Pictures
  • Veneer: Decorative plywood is made with a thin layer of natural wood veneer bonded to a plywood core. The veneer can be made from a variety of wood species, such as oak, teak, mahogany, ash, white oak, birch, maple, sapele, cherry, and beech.
  • Appearance: Decorative plywood has an aesthetically pleasing appearance and can give a warm and inviting look to any space.
  • Uses: Decorative plywood is often used for furniture, cabinetry, wall panels, and other projects where the visible surface is important.
  • Ease of use: Decorative plywood is easy to dye and draw on compared to other types of plywood.
  • Cost: Decorative plywood is more expensive than common commercial plywood, but there are affordable options available
  • Finishes: Decorative plywood is available in a range of finishes to match any decor.
  • Durability: Decorative plywood can be highly durable and termite resistant.

Leaves are generally stacked back in the same sequence that they are cut in, to help in creating a good design. The drying process then brings the moisture down to 8-10 %. The sides and ends are generally trimmed and then bundled. Each bundle consists of 24 or 32 sheets. These consecutive sheets are used for our decorative plywood. These veneers are overlaid on top of marine grade plywood to give a decorative or hard- wearing finish. The plywood we use for the base is full gurjan plywood, which is known for its durability and strength and resistance to wear and tear.
